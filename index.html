Origin Diagnostics specializes in three core modalities—CT, MRI, and X-ray— providing consistent quality and reliable interpretations for every study.

MRI

Magnetic Resonance Imaging

High-resolution imaging without radiation, ideal for brain, spine, joints, and soft tissues. Excellent detail to support confident diagnosis.

Common studies:
Brain MRI • Spine MRI • Joint MRI • Abdomen & pelvis
CT

Computed Tomography

Rapid, cross-sectional scans for trauma, lung, cardiac, and abdominal imaging. Optimized protocols balance image quality and dose.

Common studies:
CT head • CT chest • CT abdomen & pelvis • CT extremities
X-ray

Digital X-ray

Quick, low-dose digital radiography for bones, chest, and other targeted views, ideal for urgent and follow-up imaging.

Common studies:
Chest X-ray • Spine • Extremities • Pre-op screening

Preparing for your imaging exam

  • Arrive 15–20 minutes before your exam for check-in and paperwork.
  • Bring your photo ID, insurance card, and any referral or imaging orders.
  • Inform our staff if you are pregnant, may be pregnant, or breastfeeding.
  • For MRI, remove metal objects (jewelry, watches, piercings) before your exam.
  • If you’ve been told you’ll receive contrast, follow any fasting instructions you were given.

Have questions about prep, insurance coverage, or imaging options? Call our team at (281) 899-0019 or fax documents to (281) 609-9763.
